Products

You may manage your product in a single application:

  • Stock quatities based on Warehouses and stock types
  • Pricing based on price groups and time periods
  • Category based feed management system
  • Variant and attributes to service rich content products






Product Stock Management



You may manage your stock levels based on stock type and warehouse. This will give to manage different stock levels and assign customers to availability of the stock quantities.
