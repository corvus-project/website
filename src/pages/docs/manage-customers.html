Manage Customers

You may manage the customer accounts from selecting Account item in the left menu:







If you want to update any customer, click on the row. Dynamic grid allows you sort and search the customer table.









The customer API key is important for your customers to track their orders and create new orders.







From customer details page, you visit following pages related with selected customer.

  • Orders by customer
  • Edit the customer details




Customer Attributes



This is very important. You may manage the customer in different groups. These groups is allowing you to customize views for customers.

  • Pricing Group
  • Stock group
  • Warehouse


So a customer can order a product :

  • if the product is existing in warehouse and stock level is enough for ordering quantity
  • if the product has a price group that matching with customer's price group
  • if the product has a stock group that matching with customer's stock group
